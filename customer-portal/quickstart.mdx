# Get Started with Breeze Customer Portal

Get up and running with the Breeze Customer Portal in just a few simple steps. Our streamlined onboarding process ensures you can start managing your Breeze integration quickly and efficiently.


## Quick Setup Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    <Card title="Sign Up" href="https://portal.breeze.baby" icon="user-plus">
      Visit **portal.breeze.baby** to create your customer account and start integrating Breeze.
    </Card>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a strategy">
      <Card title="Strategy Creation & API Keys" href="/customer-portal/create-strategy" icon={<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" width="28" height="28" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="#bc07c9" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" class="lucide lucide-mail-icon lucide-mail"><path d="m22 7-8.991 5.727a2 2 0 0 1-2.009 0L2 7"/><rect x="2" y="4" width="20" height="16" rx="2"/></svg>}>
        Once you create an account, you'll be able to create a strategy for your application and get the API keys.
      </Card>
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're Ready!">
    <Card title="Start Managing" icon="rocket">
      Access your Customer Portal dashboard and begin managing users, monitoring performance, and optimizing your Breeze integration.
    </Card>
    
    <Tip>
    Explore the portal's features and review our comprehensive documentation to maximize your platform's potential.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
