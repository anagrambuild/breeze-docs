# Get Started with Breeze Admin Portal

Get up and running with the Breeze Admin Portal in just a few simple steps. Our streamlined onboarding process ensures you can start managing your Breeze integration quickly and efficiently.

<Info>
Complete setup typically takes 5-10 minutes, with your personal fund ready within 24 hours.
</Info>

## Quick Setup Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    <Card title="Sign Up & Get API Key" href="https://breeze.admin.com" icon="user-plus">
      Visit **breeze.admin.com** to create your admin account and receive your API key instantly upon registration.
    </Card>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Welcome Email & Team Support">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Check Your Email" icon={<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" width="28" height="28" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="#bc07c9" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" class="lucide lucide-mail-icon lucide-mail"><path d="m22 7-8.991 5.727a2 2 0 0 1-2.009 0L2 7"/><rect x="2" y="4" width="20" height="16" rx="2"/></svg>}>
        You'll receive a welcome email with detailed setup instructions, documentation links, and your account credentials.
      </Card>
      
      <Card title="Schedule Team Call" icon="calendar">
        Optional: Book a personalized onboarding call with our team for guided setup and best practices.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fund Assignment">
    <Card title="Personal On-Chain Fund" icon="coins">
      Our team will assign a dedicated on-chain fund with custom fee structures tailored to your product requirements.
    </Card>
    
    <Note>
    Fund assignment typically completes within 24 hours. You'll receive confirmation once your fund is active.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're Ready!">
    <Card title="Start Managing" icon="rocket">
      Access your Admin Portal dashboard and begin managing users, monitoring performance, and optimizing your Breeze integration.
    </Card>
    
    <Tip>
    Explore the portal's features and review our comprehensive documentation to maximize your platform's potential.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
