titel: 'Breeze-Risk-Engine (BRE)'
description: 'Risk is Dynamic, Your System Should be as well'
---

The **Breeze Risk Engine (BRE)** is designed to monitor, assess, and dynamically rebalance liquidity pools in order to offer customizable yield while maintaining robust risk management. By utilizing real-time data and advanced risk metrics, **BRE** ensures optimal capital allocation while protecting users from systemic risks such as liquidation cascades, excessive utilization, and capital inefficiencies.
