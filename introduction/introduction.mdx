# **What is Breeze?**
Breeze is the modern way to earn passive yield on your digital assets. It is a seamless non-custodial yield platform designed for easy integration with consumer applications, telegram bots, high-frequency trading platforms, MPC wallets, custodians and payments products. Breeze enables effortless yield generation across multiple assets, including Solana (SOL), USDC and LST's. By offering secure and automated staking and yield generation without the complexities of native delegation or pool management, Breeze ensures that your users never have to make a tradeoff between yield and speed.
